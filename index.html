About Me

Kryshtene Tesfaye is a claims manager with experience in adjusting residential and commercial claims as well as training and developing current insurance adjusters since 2010. After graduating with a B.S. in Applied Mathematics and completing a few computer science courses at the University of Central Arkansas, she has refocused her energy by learning full-stack web development.

When she is not working from home, she can be found in Miami Beach, Florida sun bathing on the beach, listening to music, or catching a local comedy show. Her personal motto is if you truly understand the meaning of perspective you can handle any person, place or thing!
