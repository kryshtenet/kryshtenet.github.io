TravelSmart



The idea behind our application is to make commuting easier by comparing traffic routes and deciding when is the best time to leave. We calculate routes that will get you to your destination. Our goal is to make sure that anybody who has an important business meeting, or an interview will arrive on time.

Emergency Employee Tracker


We have created a portal for companies to allow an employee a digital way of checking in during an emergency and provide updates to their staff. Employees will login daily and set their status so managers can get information on where they are working from without having to call in. Employees will be able to see company updates and messages from their managers and Human Resources. If an employee is out, they will choose a reason from a drop-down menu.
